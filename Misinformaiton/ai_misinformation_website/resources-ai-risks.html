AI Risks & Detection

Understand the technical origin of synthetic media and the risks it brings, and learn to identify the visual/time defects generated by AI.

AI Models (Understanding the Source)

GPT Models (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)

Type: Large Language Models (LLM) for text generation.

Misinformation Risk: High-quality “hallucinations” where the model confidently presents false data, non-existent citations, or fabricated historical events as absolute fact.

Stable Diffusion / GANs

Type: Diffusion and Adversarial-based image generators.

Misinformation Risk: Rapid creation of visual “proof” for events that never happened. These models excel at mimicking specific lighting and camera lenses to create a false sense of documentary realism.

Wan 2.6 (by Alibaba)

Type: High-fidelity video generation diffusion model.

Misinformation Risk: Unprecedented temporal coherence. Previous AI videos suffered from “morphing” backgrounds; Wan 2.6 maintains consistent environments, making it an ideal tool for creating false “leaked” footage or deepfake political speeches.

Spotting the “Digital Fingerprints”

Visual Artifacts: Look for asymmetrical jewelry, melting textures, or distorted limbs.
Temporal Glitches: Watch for unnatural blinking patterns or lip-sync drifting in videos.
Interactive Training: Test your eyes at MIT’s “Detect Fakes” lab.

Digital Literacy

The SIFT Method

1. STOP: When you feel a strong emotional reaction, stop before sharing.

2. INVESTIGATE THE SOURCE: Check the reputation of the site or account.

3. FIND BETTER COVERAGE: Look for lateral reading—what are other sources saying?

4. TRACE CLAIMS TO CONTEXT: Trace the media back to its original, unaltered source.

Industry Standards (C2PA)

C2PA is an open technical standard that cryptographically binds “provenance” data to a file. It allows you to see the asset’s origin (who created it), edit history (what tools were used), and whether AI was involved in the creation. By looking for the “cr” icon on images, users can verify a tamper-evident record of authenticity.
