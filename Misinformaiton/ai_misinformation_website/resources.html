01. Verification Hub

This module provides a practical tool for instant verification of media authenticity.

AI Automatic Detection: Use tools such as Deepware, Reality Defender and Hive to scan synthetic traces in video, audio and images.

Traceability Search: Reverse image search through Google Lens and TinEye to track the original source and dissemination history of the material.

Manual Verification: Quickly link to Snopes, Reuters and other professional fact-checking institutions to obtain authoritative conclusions.

02. AI and Artifacts

This module teaches you to identify the digital fingerprint of AI by analysing the technical source of synthetic media.

Understanding the Source: Learn how models such as GPT, Stable Diffusion and Wan 2.6 generate text, images and videos, and their potential false information risks.

Identify Defects: Learn to observe visual artifacts such as asymmetric jewellery or time deviations in videos such as unnatural flickering or mouth misalignment.

03. Digital Literacy

This module focusses on the establishment of a long-term thinking framework and the recognition of industry standards.

SIFT Verification Method: Master the four-step method of Stopping, Investigating, Searching and Tracing, and cultivate the habit of rationally analysing media content.

Industry Standards: Understand the C2PA content credentials protocol and learn how to identify the original author, editing history and AI participation of content through digital nutrition labelling.
